Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. DATA PRIVACY MISSION

Our suite of applications is designed as secure, "On-Device First" systems. Your personal biometrics, logs, and media are considered classified information. Our studio operates under a strict zero-knowledge privacy model.

2. INFORMATION COLLECTION & STORAGE

  • Offline Storage: All application data is stored locally on your device's internal database. We do not maintain external servers to house this information.
  • Media Assets: Photos and videos captured within our apps are stored in private directories on your device. These files are not synced to any cloud service by the studio.
  • Biometrics: Our apps utilize native System Biometric APIs. We never see or store your actual fingerprint or face data.

3. ON-DEVICE INTELLIGENCE

Advanced features such as AI meal scanning and barcode recognition are processed locally on your handset to ensure maximum data sovereignty.

4. DATA CONTROL (EXPORT & PURGE)

You maintain 100% command over your data. Within our applications, you have the right to:

  • Export: Generate CSV logs or PDF briefings for your personal use.
  • Backup: Create a local database backup for device migration.
  • Purge: Use the "SYSTEM WIPE" feature in settings to permanently delete all data from your device.
