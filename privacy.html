Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Overview & Scope

Welcome to Ace of Spades Apps. Your privacy is our single most important priority. This Privacy Policy governs your use of our corporate showroom website and our application portfolio, including Elite Vault: Hide Pro, currently available for Google Play closed beta testing.

Our studio operates under a strict zero-knowledge privacy model. We do not collect, harvest, or monetize your personal files or usage habits. All security operations occur locally on your device.

2. Website Visitors & Communications

When you visit our website or interact with our online services, minimal technical data may be processed:

  • Support Communications: If you voluntarily contact us via email (such as requesting access to a closed beta layout), we will use your email address solely to communicate with you and provide support. We never sell or share your contact information.
  • Server Logs: Like most standard websites, our hosting server may log basic technical variables (such as anonymized IP addresses and browser types) purely to ensure website security and operational uptime.

3. Application Data Storage & Encryption (Elite Vault)

The Elite Vault: Hide Pro application is built specifically as a local utility tool:

  • Local Storage: All hidden media (photos, videos, audio), documents, and personal notes are secured using military-grade AES-256 GCM encryption.
  • Data Ownership: Your encrypted database is stored EXCLUSIVELY on your physical device. We do NOT operate external cloud servers to store, backup, intercept, or view your private contents.
  • Zero Recovery Liability: Because we have no access to your device database, if you lose your Master PIN and have not previously configured the built-in Recovery Protocol, we are legally and technically unable to restore access or recover your data.

4. Android System Permissions & Usage

To enable the advanced security protocols within our application suite, the following Android runtime permissions are requested, subject to your explicit consent:

  • CAMERA: Utilized purely for security automation protocols, including the 'Intruder Selfie' and 'Spy Video' modules to capture evidence of unauthorized physical breach attempts.
  • MICROPHONE: Utilized strictly for 'Spy Audio' and voice-activated recording utilities triggered by the user.
  • STORAGE: Mandatory for locating, capturing, and encrypting files imported directly from your main device photo gallery.
  • LOCATION (GPS): Used solely to support the 'Intruder Breach Mapping' feature, which allows you to view the localized physical point where a security failure occurred. This data remains locally encrypted on your machine.
  • CONTACTS: Granted exclusively to generate and isolate a hidden contact phone directory safe within the app database structure.

5. Third-Party API Services

To ensure system stability and billing compliance on mobile marketplaces, we integrate the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing: All monetization, validation, and subscription management metrics for elite pro memberships are handled natively by Google. We do not collect, view, or retain financial credentials or bank card details.
  • Google Play Services: Core utilities utilized purely to ensure app stability monitoring, distribution, testing builds, and operational crash report collection.

6. User Controls, Deletion & Permanent Erasure

You maintain full sovereignty over your information profile:

  • Self-Destruct (Nuke PIN): Users can input an intentional erasure command which instantly wipes all local encrypted contents permanently. This action is absolute and cannot be reversed by our team.
  • Uninstallation: Deleting the software from your handset removes the local secure app storage workspace completely. Ensure you leverage the 'Export Backup' functionality prior to updating devices or deleting the application if data retention is required.

7. Children's Privacy Guidelines

As a specialized system security architecture, our services function strictly as offline device utilities. We do not knowingly harvest, process, or target operational records from children under the age of 13 or any other user tier.
