Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2024

1. DATA PRIVACY MISSION

CALORIES KING ELITE and our suite of applications are designed as secure, "On-Device First" systems. Your personal biometrics, nutritional logs, and progress media are considered classified information. Our studio operates under a strict zero-knowledge privacy model.

2. INFORMATION COLLECTION & STORAGE

  • Offline Storage: All calorie logs, body measurements (Weight, Waist, etc.), and nutritional data are stored locally on your device's internal database. We do not maintain external servers to house this data.
  • Progress Media (Vault): Photos and videos captured within the "Progress Vault" are stored in a private directory on your device. These files are not synced to any cloud service by the app.
  • Biometrics: The app utilizes Android's System Biometric API for Vault access. The app never sees or stores your actual fingerprint or face data; it only receives a "Success" or "Failure" signal from the Android system.

3. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

  • Barcode Scanning: The app uses Google ML Kit for barcode recognition. This process happens entirely on your device.
  • AI Meal Scanning: Pro users utilizing AI Meal Scanning send image data to a secure processing API to identify nutritional values. This data is used only for the immediate nutritional lookup and is not stored or sold.

4. DATA CONTROL (EXPORT & PURGE)

You maintain 100% command over your data. Within our applications, you have the right to:

  • Export: Generate CSV logs or PDF briefings for your personal use.
  • Backup: Create a local database backup for migration purposes.
  • Purge: Use the "SYSTEM WIPE" or "PURGE" feature in settings to permanently delete all data from your device.
