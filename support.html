SYSTEM KNOWLEDGE ADVISORIES

How do I migrate my data to a new device?

Navigate to Settings > Data & Backup Tools and select "Create Full Backup." This generates a .db file that you can transfer to your new device. On the new device, use the "Restore from Backup" option to re-import your profile.

How does the AI Meal Scanning work?

Our AI engine analyzes your meal description or photo to extract nutritional assets locally and via secure lookup. Pro users receive 5 daily scans which reset automatically at midnight.

Is my data stored on Ace of Spades servers?

No. We utilize a zero-knowledge architecture. Your calorie logs, body measurements, and progress media are stored exclusively in your device's private sandbox.
