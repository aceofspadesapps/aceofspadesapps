SYSTEM KNOWLEDGE ADVISORIES

How do I migrate my data to a new device?
Navigate to Settings > Data & Backup Tools and select "Create System Backup." This generates a .db file that you can transfer to your new device. On the new device, use the "Restore from Backup" option to re-import your profile.
How does the AI Meal Scanning work?
Our AI engine utilizes on-device neural processing to identify nutritional values. All scanning is UNLIMITED and handled entirely on your handset to ensure absolute privacy.
Is my data stored on Ace of Spades servers?
No. We utilize a zero-knowledge architecture. Your calorie logs, body measurements, and progress media are stored exclusively in your device's private sandbox. We maintain zero external servers for user data.
